The Experience

The Krash experience is like living in a particle accelerator for people. Beyond the optimal living environment, both the houses and the programming are designed to maximize collaboration, innovation and the establishment of life-long friendships.

Network

Being inside the Krash community gives you access to exceptional people worldwide. Immerse yourself in our weekly dinners, learning programs and outings, or kick back with leading founders, CEOs and investors. As a Krasher you are one of a hand-selected global membership of leaders.

Epicenter

Krash houses are strategically located at the cross-section of the start-up, innovation and investment communities in each city. We love public transportation and make it easy for you to get to major venues, bars, restaurants, shops and community spaces in the surrounding neighborhoods.
